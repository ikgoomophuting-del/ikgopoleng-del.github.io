About Me

I’m Ikgopoleng Mophuting, an aspiring Junior Data Scientist passionate about turning complex problems into actionable insights. Growing up in Sedibeng, I witnessed how a lack of reliable data can lead to inefficiencies in vital public services like healthcare. This inspired me to pursue data science to bridge the gap between raw data and smarter decision-making.

Why Data Science?

Data science gives me the tools to make sense of real-world problems and create smarter, faster ways to solve them. It’s not just about technology — it’s about making meaningful changes in communities like mine by using insights to improve services.

Career Vision

My career starts with a role as a Junior Data Scientist, building experience with data analysis and visualization. Over time, I aim to grow into a senior data role and help organizations use data-driven insights to improve decision-making and create better systems for communities and businesses alike.
